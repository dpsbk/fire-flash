about this project

Fire Flash is a website created by Sarthak Sidhant, a student in class 10/A at DPS Bokaro, for the Code-Flash competition. The website aims to share information about fire ecology in a structured and styled manner.

The Website Features: Information Presented Through Text, Articles, Videos and Images. Charity for burning Maui. Animals that Adapted, About the Website, Frequently asked questions. Fiery Quotes. Data Driven Analysis.

Fire ecology is the study of the role of fire in ecosystems.
 Fire is a natural process that has shaped the landscape of many parts of the world. It can be destructive, but it can also be beneficial. For example, fire can help to clear away dead and diseased vegetation, which can help to prevent the spread of larger, more destructive fires. Fire can also recycle nutrients back into the soil and create a more diverse habitat for plants and animals.

The Fire Flash website is designed to be easy to use and informative. The information is presented in a clear and concise way, with plenty of visuals to help illustrate the concepts. 

I hope you find the Fire Flash website to be a valuable resource for learning about fire ecology.
